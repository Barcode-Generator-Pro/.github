## 🚀 Overview

Barcode Generator Pro is a robust and versatile barcode maker for macOS, designed for users who need precision, variety, and export flexibility. If you work with inventory, shipping labels, retail, publishing, or packaging, this tool gives you control over barcode types, styles, colors, captions, and output formats.  

You can generate 1-D barcodes (Code 3 of 9, Code 128, Interleaved 2 of 5, Industrial 2 of 5, UPC, EAN, Codabar, etc.) and also use advanced options like customizing font on the caption, adjusting color, and adding or removing captions. Before exporting, you can preview how the barcode looks: you can zoom, adjust width, height, margin, and alignment to ensure scanning reliability.  

Exporting is powerful: PDF (vector quality for print), EPS, TIFF, and high resolution raster formats are supported. This makes it ideal both for on-screen use and print materials. You also get clipboard copy, batch-ready workflows (if generating many codes at once), and automatic rendering of captions under or aside the code.  

The user interface is intuitive: simple panel to choose barcode symbology, input the data, adjust aesthetic and label settings, preview in real time, and then export. Whether you're labeling products, creating custom tags, or printing labels, Barcode Generator Pro is made to produce professional barcodes without a steep learning curve.
